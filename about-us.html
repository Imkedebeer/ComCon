More about our Team...

Above you see the talented team behind ComCon, CompanionConnects! We are a group of four innovative Creative Business students with the mission to empower elderly individuals living alone. We aim to take away their technology-related challenges and create a community with our users.

Through dedicated work, we gained a thorough understanding of the difficulties this generation is facing. We developed several workshops and personal tech services provided at home, tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual. From learning how to navigate social media to get online access to important services, ComCon is here to help every step of the way.

The workshops are designed to be accessible, affordable and effective, given by passionate technology experts. They will take the time to understand each of our customer’s specific goals an challenges. Don’t worry there is also the option to have a cup of tea afterwards!

For our users to get the most out of our services, we also created a vibrant community. We understand the importance of social connections, especially for those who may be living alone. It enables our customers to meet and connect with others who share their interests and challenges. Join our community to access a supportive environment to not just learn from us but also from each other!
